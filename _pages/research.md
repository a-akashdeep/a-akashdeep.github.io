My research lies at the intersection of experimental condensed matter physics, spintronics, magnetic thin films, and emerging magnetic materials. I am especially interested in how magnetic order, interfaces, surfaces, crystal symmetry, and spin-orbit coupling influence transport and dynamics in heterostructures.

## Altermagnetism and RuO₂-based systems

A major part of my research focuses on RuO₂ and related altermagnetic systems. This work investigates how compensated magnetic order and symmetry can influence spin transport, magnetoresistance, and spectroscopic signatures.

Related topics:

- RuO₂ thin films and heterostructures
- Altermagnetic spin transport
- Interface-generated spin currents
- Surface-localized magnetic order
- Angle-dependent magnetoresistance
- Time-reversal symmetry breaking

## Spin currents and magnetotransport

I have worked on transport-based studies of magnetic heterostructures, including spin-current-related magnetoresistance, Hall-related effects, and temperature-, field-, and angle-dependent measurements.

Related topics:

- Magnetotransport
- Spin Hall and inverse spin Hall effects
- Anomalous Hall and topological Hall-like signals
- Temperature-dependent transport
- Magnetic-field-dependent measurements

## Ferrimagnetic garnets and magnonics

My work also includes ferrimagnetic garnet systems such as YIG/GdIG/Pt and rare-earth iron garnets. These systems are useful for studying spin dynamics, spin pumping, spin Seebeck effects, compensation regimes, and magnon transport.

Related topics:

- Ferromagnetic resonance
- Spin pumping
- Spin Seebeck effect
- Magnon transport
- Compensation effects
- Ferrimagnetic spin dynamics

## Experimental methods and infrastructure

Across my projects, I have gained experience with thin-film growth, characterization, fabrication, low-temperature transport, beamline experiments, and lab instrumentation.

Methods and tools include:

- DC/RF/pulsed magnetron sputtering
- PLD, RHEED, thermal evaporation, chemical/spin coating
- XRD/XRR, SQUID/VSM, MOKE, FMR, AFM
- Electrical and magnetotransport measurements at low temperature and high magnetic field
- Optical and e-beam lithography, ion etching, annealing, wire bonding
- Low-energy muon spectroscopy
- XMCD-PEEM and X-ray-based characterization
- Python, LabVIEW, OriginPro, LaTeX, Inkscape, SolidWorks, and K-layout
