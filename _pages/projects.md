# Projects

This page presents selected projects from my research, technical, teaching, and coordination experience. I use this page as an extended CV: a place to show work that may not fit into a short job-specific CV but helps explain what I have done, how I work, and what skills I bring.

## Research projects

### RuO₂/permalloy spin-current magnetoresistance

**Summary:**  
Investigated angle-dependent magnetoresistance and interface-generated spin-current effects in RuO₂/permalloy heterostructures.

**What this project shows:**  
Spintronics research, magnetotransport, experimental interpretation, and the ability to separate competing physical mechanisms.

**Methods:**  
Magnetotransport, angle-dependent measurements, temperature-dependent measurements, magnetic-field-dependent analysis, thin-film heterostructures.

**Related publication:**  
Akashdeep et al., *Physical Review Applied* 24, 054018 (2025).

---

### Surface-localized magnetic order in RuO₂ thin films

**Summary:**  
Studied surface-localized magnetic order in RuO₂ thin films using low-energy muon probes.

**What this project shows:**  
Advanced characterization, collaboration, depth-sensitive magnetic analysis, and interpretation of complex magnetic signals.

**Methods:**  
Low-energy muon spectroscopy, thin-film systems, temperature-dependent analysis, collaborative facility-based research.

**Related output:**  
Akashdeep et al., arXiv:2510.08064.

---

### Ferrimagnetic garnet spin dynamics

**Summary:**  
Studied temperature-dependent spin dynamics and spin-current generation in YIG/GdIG/Pt trilayers.

**What this project shows:**  
Experience with ferrimagnetic systems, FMR-based analysis, spin dynamics, and magnonics.

**Methods:**  
Ferromagnetic resonance, spin pumping, spin Seebeck effect, inverse spin Hall detection, temperature-dependent measurements.

**Related publication:**  
Fuhrmann et al., *Physical Review B* 112, 064432 (2025).
